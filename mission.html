Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that no life is lost due to delays in accessing blood.
We aim to create a smart, inclusive blood donation network that:

  • Empowers citizens to donate through simple, everyday tools
  • Supports hospitals with real-time access to nearby donors
  • Improves Rwanda’s blood supply chain with data-driven systems
  • Promotes regular donation by removing information and location barriers

Together, we’re building a future where every willing donor can respond when it matters most.
