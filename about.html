About Us

Save Life is a digital health initiative built to bridge the gap between blood donors and hospitals in Rwanda. Using simple technologies like USSD and SMS, we help connect willing donors with hospitals in need — instantly, reliably, and without the need for internet or smartphones.

Our platform was designed with one goal in mind: save lives by making blood donation easier, faster, and more accessible for everyone. Whether you're in Kigali or a remote village, Save Life ensures that no patient suffers due to a lack of timely blood access.

We are driven by community, powered by technology, and focused on impact.
